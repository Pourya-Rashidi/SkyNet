Revolutionizing Astronomy

Use Cases

  • Student and university research projects
  • STEM education for schools
  • Global public outreach events
  • Citizen science and amateur astronomer collaboration
  • Monitoring of asteroids, satellites, and near-Earth objects
  • Deep sky object imaging for enthusiasts
  • Time-lapse sky surveys and variable star studies

Membership & Access

SkyNet will adopt a token-based system for membership and telescope time allocation. Supporters, researchers, and educational partners can obtain access by staking or acquiring tokens. Token holders will enjoy:

  • Priority scheduling for observations
  • Voting on future telescope installations
  • Exclusive educational resources and workshops
