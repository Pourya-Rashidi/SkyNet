    About SkyNet

    SkyNet is an international project that brings the wonders of the universe closer to everyone, regardless of location, background, or expertise. We provide real-time access to robotic telescopes and satellite data, enabling users around the world to observe the sky, track planetary changes, and participate in meaningful scientific discovery.

    Our mission is to democratize astronomy by involving students, educators, amateurs, and professionals — from teenagers to mid-career scientists — in space science and environmental monitoring. We believe that the future of science lies in open access, collaboration, and curiosity-driven learning.

    SkyNet monitors Earth’s evolving climate through satellite imagery and remote sensing, contributing to a deeper understanding of our planet's health and future. Our tools also support research into the solar system, asteroids, and the ongoing search for habitable exoplanets.

    This is more than just a science project — it’s a movement to inspire, educate, and connect. By combining technology with passion, we aim to expand our knowledge of the cosmos while also helping improve life on Earth.

    As SkyNet reaches beyond borders, this phase ignites global collaboration—through funding, grants, and strategic alliances. It’s not just about resources, but about uniting those who believe the sky belongs to everyone. Together, we lay the foundation for a future shaped by open science and shared vision. Join us in building a global community that explores space, protects our planet, and inspires the next generation of thinkers and dreamers.
