Phase I: Idea & Vision
The concept, mission, and vision for SkyNet as a global online observatory and climate monitoring network were clarified.
Phase II: Website & Branding
Launching the first version of the SkyNet website on GitHub. Developing identity, logo, and initial content.
Phase III: Core Team Formation
Outlining initial organizational structure, Initial outreach via LinkedIn group, Recruiting passionate contributors.
Phase IV: Global Funding & Partnerships
Seeking international funding, launching crowdfunding, applying for grants, and building partnerships with institutions.
Phase V: Prototype Development
Designing initial telescope prototype using ESP8266. Setting up remote access and simple controls.
Phase VI: Pilot Testing
Testing prototype functionality. Collecting user feedback and identifying technical limitations.
Phase VII: Infrastructure Expansion
Building network of telescopes, cloud data infrastructure, and integration with climate sensors.
Phase VIII: Public Beta Launch
Opening platform to early public access. Community building and global feedback collection.
Phase IX: Education & Global Outreach
Collaborating with schools, NGOs, and space organizations to deliver education and citizen science opportunities.
Phase X: Sustainability & Climate Action
Long-term operation of SkyNet for astronomical research and climate change monitoring. Promoting global sustainability efforts.
